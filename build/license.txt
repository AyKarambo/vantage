WHAT VANTAGE IS

Vantage is a free Windows desktop app that turns your own Overwatch match history
into a personal stats dashboard. It is an independent project: it is not
affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Blizzard Entertainment, Overwatch,
or Overwolf. All game names, marks, and assets belong to their respective owners.

The application's source is released under the MIT License. It is free for
personal use. You may not resell it or misrepresent its origin.


YOUR DATA STAYS ON YOUR PC

Vantage reads match information only through Overwolf's official Game Events
Provider - the sanctioned events feed. That covers only information for your own
account that you can already see in your own game: map, game mode, role, hero,
match result, duration, and your own end-of-match scoreboard.

Vantage does not read game memory, does not inject into the game, and does not
access hidden or other-player information.

Your match history, your manual notes, and your settings are stored locally on
your computer only. The developer operates no server and does not receive your
gameplay data. Uninstalling the app, or deleting its user-data folder, removes it.

There is no account system: no name, email, or password is collected. The app
shows no advertising. Your data is never sold or rented.


THE TWO THINGS THAT DO LEAVE YOUR PC

1. Notion export - OFF unless you turn it on. If you connect a Notion
   integration, Vantage sends your tracked games to a Notion database that you
   own, using a token you provide. The token is stored encrypted at rest on your
   PC. Notion is an independent service governed by its own privacy policy. You
   can disconnect at any time, which deletes the token.

2. Platform analytics - Vantage runs on Overwolf's ow-electron platform, and
   Overwolf collects anonymous app-usage analytics by default to measure
   installs, retention, and performance. This processing is carried out by
   Overwolf under Overwolf's privacy policy. It does not include your match data.
