Privacy Policy

Vantage · Effective 2 July 2026

Vantage is a Windows desktop application that turns your Overwatch match history into a personal stats dashboard. This policy explains exactly what data the app touches, where it is stored, and who (if anyone) it is shared with. In short: your match data stays on your own PC. The developer operates no server and does not receive your gameplay data.

1. Who we are

Vantage is an independent app created by Timo Seikel ("we", "us"). It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Blizzard Entertainment, Overwatch, or Overwolf. Contact: timo.seikel@gmail.com.

2. What data the app processes

  • Overwatch match data. Vantage reads match information through Overwolf's official Game Events Provider (GEP) — the sanctioned events feed. This covers only information for your own account that you can already see in your own game: map, game mode, role, hero, match result, match duration, and your own end-of-match scoreboard (eliminations, deaths, assists, damage, healing, mitigation). Vantage does not read game memory, does not inject into the game, and does not access any hidden or other-player information.
  • Information you enter manually. Optional after-game notes such as tilt / communication / leaver flags and improvement targets that you create yourself.
  • App settings. Your preferences (e.g. tracked accounts, map name aliases, run-at-login).

3. Where it is stored

All of the above is stored locally on your computer only, in your Windows user-data folder (files such as history.json, outbox.json, and config.local.json). It is not uploaded to us and it is not shared with any third party except in the optional cases described below. Deleting these files, or uninstalling the app, removes the data.

4. Optional Notion export

If you choose to connect a Notion integration, Vantage sends your tracked games to a Notion database that you own, using an integration token you provide. That token is stored encrypted at rest on your PC (via the operating system's secure storage). Notion is an independent third-party service; data you export there is governed by Notion's Privacy Policy. This feature is off unless you connect it, and you can disconnect at any time by clearing the token, which deletes it from your PC.

5. Platform analytics (Overwolf)

Vantage runs on Overwolf's ow-electron platform. Overwolf collects anonymous app-usage analytics by default to measure installs, retention, and performance. This processing is carried out by Overwolf and is governed by Overwolf's Privacy Policy. It does not include your match data.

6. What we do not do

  • We do not sell or rent your data.
  • We show no advertising in the app.
  • We have no account system and collect no name, email, or password.

7. Your control

Because your data lives on your own machine, you are always in control: delete the local data files or uninstall the app to erase it, and clear the Notion token to stop any export. For questions or requests, email timo.seikel@gmail.com.

8. Children

Vantage is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

9. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by a new effective date at the top of this page.
